Day 3: Lessons at Hotel Conference Room

A study of William Walton's prelude to Richard III (1 hour) and a study of how setting can impact plot (Richard III, R&J) (1 hour).

Visit the National Portrait Gallery, where students will use the website to access a choice board. While enjoying the museum, they will visit approximately 3-5 pieces of choice, each featuring audio explaining how it relates to one of the mentor texts and music that links to the same text. Workbooks will have space for students to respond to journal prompts specific to the paintings.
Creation Space

Students will rewrite a scene from Romeo and Juliet in a new context (90 minutes).

City Bus Photo Bingo - take students on a double-decker bus to complete a photo bingo challenge. Choose a route that passes through Westminster and Trafalgar Square and ends at an easy place for dining. (11, 9, 24)
Reflection time at the hotel, discussing insights gained from the day’s activities.
