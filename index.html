Shakespeare's London: A Literary Adventure

Join us for an unforgettable educational journey to London in the summer of 2025. This unique trip offers students the opportunity to immerse themselves in the world of William Shakespeare and explore his profound impact on literature, drama, and music.

Walk in the footsteps of the Bard himself as we visit historical sites, attend performances, and engage in interactive workshops. From the reconstructed Globe Theatre to the bustling streets of modern London, students will gain a deep appreciation for Shakespeare's enduring legacy.

This trip is designed to bring Shakespeare's works to life, offering a perfect blend of academic enrichment and cultural exploration. Don't miss this chance to experience the magic of Shakespeare in the very city where his genius flourished!

Key Destinations

  • Shakespeare's Globe Theatre
  • Stratford-upon-Avon (Shakespeare's birthplace)
  • The Royal Shakespeare Company
  • The British Library (to view original manuscripts)
  • Westminster Abbey (Poets' Corner)
  • The National Theatre
  • The Victoria and Albert Museum
  • A West End performance of a Shakespeare play
